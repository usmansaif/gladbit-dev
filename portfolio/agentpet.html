AgentPet

Pet Marketplace & Accessories eCommerce | Lahore, Pakistan

About the Project

AgentPet is an online platform that enables users to buy and sell pets, browse pet accessories and food, and find nearby pet stores. In addition to product listings and forums, the platform supports pet relocation services to assist owners in managing their pets’ logistics efficiently.

Key Features

  • - Buy and sell pets with verified listings
  • - eCommerce shop for accessories and pet food
  • - Pet relocation service options
  • - Geo-based store finder for local shops
  • - Community forum for pet owners

Technology Used

Laravel PHP MySQL jQuery Bootstrap GitHub
