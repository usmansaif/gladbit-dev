Client

PakPoultry.pk is a digital platform serving thousands of poultry traders, farmers, and retailers across Pakistan. They focus on providing reliable poultry price information to ensure fair transactions in the competitive poultry market.

The Challenge

In the poultry industry, timely and accurate pricing is crucial. However, the traditional methods of collecting and sharing prices were inefficient:

  • Inconsistent, delayed, and unreliable pricing shared informally through phone calls, WhatsApp, or social media.
  • Manual effort required each day to gather rates for day-old chicks, eggs, broiler chickens, and farm produce.
  • Limited geographic reach for price information, often not targeting specific regions or cities.

PakPoultry.pk needed to create a fully automated system to provide accurate pricing information to their subscribers, directly to their mobile phones, in real time.
