Case Study: Enhancing Delivery Accuracy with OTP-Based Food Order Confirmation for Whatapratha

Ensuring the right food reaches the right customer through a secure and efficient OTP verification system.

  • Client
  • The Challenge
  • Our Solution
  • Results & Impact

Client

Whatapratha is a popular food brand known for its delicious offerings and timely home deliveries. As the business expanded, maintaining high delivery accuracy became essential for ensuring customer satisfaction and minimizing operational errors.

The Challenge

Whatapratha faced occasional delivery errors that resulted in:

  • Orders being delivered to the wrong address or picked up by the wrong person, leading to customer dissatisfaction.
  • Reliance on verbal confirmations during peak hours, leading to miscommunication, especially in shared living spaces.
  • Difficulty in verifying each delivery without adding complexity to the process.

To improve accuracy and minimize errors, Whatapratha needed a solution that could confirm the delivery with minimal disruption to their existing processes.

Our Solution

GladBit developed a custom OTP-based Android application tailored to Whatapratha's internal team to streamline the delivery verification process:
