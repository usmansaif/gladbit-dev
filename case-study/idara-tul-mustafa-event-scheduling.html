Case Study: Streamlining Event Scheduling for Idara Tul Mustafa

A custom web application to simplify and automate event management for an Islamic organization.

  • Client
  • The Challenge
  • Our Solution
  • Results & Impact

Client

Idara Tul Mustafa is a prominent Islamic organization that hosts spiritual and religious gatherings with scholars across various regions. With a growing number of events, they faced challenges in managing the logistics of event scheduling manually.

The Challenge

As the number of events increased, the team struggled with managing schedules, leading to:

  • Double-bookings and scheduling conflicts between scholars for different events.
  • Lack of centralized event tracking to keep a record of all past and upcoming events.
  • Time-consuming coordination efforts with phone calls, handwritten schedules, and WhatsApp messages.
  • Inconsistent communication leading to missed details or confusion during the scheduling process.

They needed a reliable, easy-to-use digital solution to eliminate scheduling errors and enhance coordination.

Our Solution

GladBit developed a custom event planner web application built on Laravel to meet the organization's specific needs:

With the custom event planner, Idara Tul Mustafa experienced significant improvements:
