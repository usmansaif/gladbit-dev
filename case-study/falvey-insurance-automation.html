Case Study: Automating Insurance Integrations for Faster Shipping

A custom solution for Falvey Insurance that streamlines insurance workflows for logistics platforms

  • Client Challenges
  • Our Solution
  • The Results

Challenges Faced by the Client

Our client, a major logistics provider, faced significant difficulties in integrating with Falvey Insurance’s complex system. The process to insure shipments and track claims was cumbersome and error-prone, requiring manual entry and numerous back-and-forth communications. The lack of automation resulted in increased processing time and errors, which delayed shipments and led to customer dissatisfaction.

  • Manual data entry and multiple steps to insure shipments.
  • High risk of errors in claim submission and updates.
  • Significant delays in processing times, impacting overall shipping efficiency.

Our Custom Solution

At GladBit, we specialize in creating seamless software integrations. We built a custom connector between our client’s platform and Falvey Insurance to automate the entire insurance process, removing the need for manual intervention.

  • Instant Access to Rates: Real-time fetching of insurance pricing for any shipment value.
  • One-Click Claim Submission: Automated claim submission, eliminating the hours of manual effort.
  • Live Status Updates: Continuous updates on shipments and claim status, without requiring user interaction.
  • Testing and Live Mode: Thorough testing in Falvey’s sandbox environment, followed by a smooth transition to live operations.

The Results Achieved

The integration we delivered not only streamlined operations but also provided tangible improvements in efficiency and accuracy. Our solution saved the client valuable time, reduced errors, and improved overall service delivery.

  • Reduced Errors: Automated data entry eliminated costly mistakes.
  • Time Savings: Processes that once took hours are now completed in seconds.
  • Seamless Integration: The insurance process runs automatically behind the scenes, without requiring any disruption to existing operations.
  • Future-Proof Solution: The system is scalable, ensuring it can grow alongside the client's expanding business.
