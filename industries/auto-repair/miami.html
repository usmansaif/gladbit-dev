Custom Auto Repair Software Development in Miami

Managing an auto repair business in today’s fast-paced market is challenging. From tracking customer vehicles to managing technicians, invoices, and inventory — every process demands precision and time. That’s where GladBit helps you take control.

We specialize in building custom auto repair software solutions designed for garages, mechanic shops, and automotive service centers in Miami . Our scalable systems automate your workflow, improve accuracy, and help you deliver better customer experiences — every time.

Challenges Auto Repair Shops Commonly Face

  • Manual scheduling causes double-bookings or missed appointments.
  • Technician tasks and job progress are hard to monitor in real-time.
  • Invoicing, estimates, and payments are slow or inconsistent.
  • Customer and vehicle data get lost between spreadsheets and paper forms.
  • Inventory tracking and parts ordering lack visibility and automation.

Our Solution — Built Around Your Workflow

GladBit creates custom software for auto repair businesses that replaces disconnected tools with one unified system. Whether you need a shop management dashboard, customer booking portal, or automation for invoices and reminders — we design solutions that fit your business perfectly.

Key Features of Our Auto Repair Software

Job Scheduling & Tracking

Schedule jobs, assign technicians, and track progress in real-time with an intuitive dashboard.

Customer & Vehicle Management

Maintain complete service histories, vehicle data, and communication logs in one secure place.

Invoicing & Payment Integration

Generate accurate estimates and invoices with built-in payment gateway and accounting integrations.

Inventory & Parts Management

Monitor stock levels, order parts automatically, and reduce delays with intelligent inventory tracking.

Performance Reports

Gain insights into sales, technician performance, and operational efficiency with real-time analytics.

Automation Workflows

Automate reminders, follow-ups, and customer notifications to save time and boost retention.
