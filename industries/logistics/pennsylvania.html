Logistics Software Solutions for Pennsylvania

At GladBit, we build custom software for logistics, transportation, and supply chain businesses that helps you manage shipments, fleets, warehouse operations, and deliveries efficiently. Whether you operate in Pennsylvania or nationwide, our tailored digital solutions streamline your operations and boost efficiency.

Common Challenges in Logistics Management

  • Difficulty tracking shipments and deliveries in real-time.
  • Manual handling of inventory and warehouse operations.
  • Lack of centralized system for fleet and route management.
  • Poor visibility into performance metrics, costs, and efficiency.

Our Software Solutions

  • Fleet Management System – Track vehicles, drivers, and routes efficiently.
  • Shipment & Delivery Tracking – Monitor shipments in real-time from origin to destination.
  • Warehouse & Inventory Management – Automate stock tracking, orders, and warehouse workflow.
  • Logistics CRM – Manage clients, contracts, and operational communication centrally.
  • Analytics Dashboard – Gain insights into costs, delivery performance, and operational efficiency.

Technologies We Use

Our solutions are built using modern frameworks and technologies for performance and scalability:

  • Vue.js, React.js (Frontend for Dashboards)
  • Laravel (Backend for Fleet & Warehouse Management)
  • REST & GraphQL APIs
  • Database Optimization & Tracking Systems
  • Automation for Routes, Inventory & Deliveries
