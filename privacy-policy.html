Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2025

At GladBit, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and handling it responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and enhance our services, including:

  • Personal Information: Information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, and company details when you contact us, sign up, or fill out forms.
  • Usage Data: Information automatically collected when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, device details, and pages visited, to help us improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Delivering and improving our software services.
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support.
  • Processing transactions and sending service-related updates.
  • Analyzing website traffic and usage trends for optimization.
  • Ensuring compliance with legal requirements and protecting our rights.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data only in these cases:

  • With Trusted Service Providers: Such as payment processors, email services, and analytics providers, strictly to support our business operations.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the safety and rights of GladBit and its users.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures — including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers — to safeguard your personal data. While we strive to protect your information, no system can guarantee complete security. Please take necessary precautions when sharing sensitive information online.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze traffic, and customize content. You can control cookie preferences via your browser settings; however, disabling cookies may affect some website features.

6. Links to Third-Party Sites

Our site may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing personal data.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, where applicable.
  • Opt out of marketing emails and communications at any time.

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at hello@gladbit.com.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updated versions will be posted here with the latest revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us at:

Email: hello@gladbit.com
